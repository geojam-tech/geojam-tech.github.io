GeoJam

A hands-on community at the intersection of geospatial data and AI.

GeoJam runs hackathons, workshops, and experiments for people who work with spatial data and modern AI. Whether you come from GIS, remote sensing, data science, or ML engineering — if you want to tinker with geospatial problems and learn by building, this is your space.
